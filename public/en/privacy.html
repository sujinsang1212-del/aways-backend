2. Purpose of Collection and Use

Collected personal information is used only for the following purposes:

  1. Service Provision: PDF Report delivery, sending download links
  2. Payment and Refund Processing: Payment confirmation, refund request processing, transaction record management
  3. Customer Support: Responding to inquiries, sending notices
  4. Service Improvement: Access statistics analysis, service quality enhancement (used in non-identifiable form)

3. Retention and Use Period

The Company destroys personal information without delay after the purpose of collection and use is achieved. However, the following information is retained for the period prescribed by law:

Records on contracts or withdrawal of subscriptions Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce 5 years
Records on payment and supply of goods Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce 5 years

Access logs Protection of Communications Secrets Act 3 months

4. Provision to Third Parties

The Company does not provide Users' personal information to external parties as a general rule. However, the following are exceptions:

6. User Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  1. Right to Access: Confirm how personal information is being processed
  2. Right to Rectification or Erasure: Correct or delete inaccurate information
  3. Right to Suspension of Processing: Temporarily or permanently suspend personal information processing
  4. Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent to collection and use

These rights can be exercised by emailing awaysbiz@gmail.com, and the Company will take action without delay.

7. Destruction Procedures and Methods

  1. Destruction Procedure: Personal information whose purpose has been achieved is moved to a separate database and stored for a certain period in accordance with internal policies and relevant laws before being destroyed.
  2. Destruction Method:

8. Cookie Operation and Refusal

The Company uses cookies to provide customized services to Users.

  1. Purpose of Cookies: Access frequency analysis, service improvement
  2. How to Refuse Cookies: You can refuse cookie storage in your browser settings.
    • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
    • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
    • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  3. Refusing cookie storage may cause difficulties in using some services.

9. Security Measures

The Company takes the following measures to protect personal information:

  1. Encrypted storage and transmission of personal information (HTTPS applied)

  4. Minimization and regular training of personnel handling personal information
