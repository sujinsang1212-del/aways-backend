7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I get a refund after downloading the Report?

Yes. Within 7 days, refunds are available regardless of whether you've downloaded the Report. However, we ask that you delete the downloaded file upon refund.

Q. Do I have to provide a reason for the refund?

No, providing a reason is optional. Simple change of mind is acceptable. However, your feedback would greatly help us improve our service.

Q. How can I check the refund status?

We'll send a confirmation email when your refund request is received and another notification when processing is complete.

Q. Are refunds really impossible after 7 days?

In principle, refunds after 7 days are difficult. However, if there's a defect in the Report itself or the issue is the Company's fault, refunds are available regardless of the time period. Please contact us by email first.
