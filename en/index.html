The Six Types

Which one are you?

Each type has its own line, fabric, and silhouette logic. Most online quizzes give you one of three. We give you one of six.

Soft Wave
Feminine curves, delicate frame. Soft fabrics and fitted silhouettes flatter most.
Hard Wave
Curves with stronger structure. Defined waistlines and crisp materials work best.
Soft Straight
Linear lines, softer presence. Drapey fabrics and clean cuts elevate the look.
Hard Straight
Bold, sculpted lines. Sharp tailoring and rich, structured fabrics suit you.
Soft Natural
Relaxed frame, gentle edges. Casual layering and natural textures look effortless.
Hard Natural
Strong, athletic frame. Bold silhouettes and statement pieces match your energy.
